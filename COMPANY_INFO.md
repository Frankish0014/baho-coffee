# Company Information Configuration

## Update These Details in Quotation Email

To make the quotation emails fully professional, please update the following placeholders in:
`app/api/export/quotation/route.ts`

### Company Details to Update:

1. **TIN (Tax Identification Number)**
   - Current: `TIN: [To be provided]`
   - Replace with your actual Rwanda TIN number

2. **Registration Number**
   - Current: `Reg. No: [To be provided]`
   - Replace with your company registration number from RDB (Rwanda Development Board)

3. **Phone Number**
   - Current: `+250 XXX XXX XXX`
   - Replace with your actual business phone number
   - Also update in Footer component if needed

4. **Physical Address** (Optional)
   - Add complete address if needed
   - Current: `Kigali, Rwanda`

5. **Bank Details** (Optional - for final quotation)
   - Can be added to the detailed quotation when sent
